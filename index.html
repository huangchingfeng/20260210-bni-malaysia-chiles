Core Concepts

5 Key Takeaways from This Course

01

The Pilot Mindset

You are the pilot, AI is your crew. The pilot is important and earns good money, but the pilot doesn't serve meals or clean toilets. AI is your team - you need to understand what AI can help you solve.

02

Use the Right Tool

You can't ask ChatGPT to make slides, just like you can't use Excel for presentations. It's not the tool's fault - you chose the wrong tool. Each AI tool has its specialty.

03

Free Tools Are Enough

Most people's AI skills are like your grandmother using an iPhone. Is there a difference between iPhone 17 Pro Max and a 2000 MYR Oppo for her? No.

04

Essential Skills for AI Era

AI will become like mobile internet - you can't possibly not know how to use it. It's not about knowing vs not knowing, but knowing vs being an expert. Become an expert!

05

Websites for AI

Future websites aren't for humans to browse - they're for AI. Your website needs more text; beautiful images mean nothing if AI can't read the text. 20% of US Black Friday traffic already comes from AI.
